FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What types of documents can you notarize?

I can notarize documents that require notarization, including affidavits, powers of attorney, estate planning paperwork, and many loan or business documents.

Are there documents you cannot notarize?

Yes. Vital records such as birth or marriage certificates cannot be notarized, and I cannot notarize if the signer lacks valid identification or proper capacity.

What payment methods do you accept?

Cash, Venmo, Zelle, and PayPal are accepted. If another arrangement works better, I’m happy to discuss it.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can call, text, or email me to set up a time that works for you. Same-day appointments are often available.
